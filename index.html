Welcome!

We offer a broad range of agro livestock products. We create self-made enterpreneurs.

Since long time, the agriculture and livestock entrepreneurs has been facing procurement problems of quality services and production input to improve production and productivity of such enterprise to obtain maximum profit. Realizing the above fact, Sankalpa Agrotech came into existence, aiming to accelerate business development services to entrepreneur farmers for sustainable, impactful,social and economic transformation through reliable technical linkage, animal insurance and value chain development.

Our programs:

  • Cattel rearing training
