Our Milestones

MOU between cooperatives

ManaMuri Cooperative(Manohara,Kathmandu) and Sankalpa Agrotech PVT. LTD. have signed the business partnership agreement on meat value chain marketing. The live he-goat business value chain business model is designed and implemented on the basis of live weighing system.The partnership agreement is a continuous effort to support proverty elimination of distant producer's and marketing facilities of the livestock sector in Nepal. Especially farmers, entrepreneurs will be offered and opportunities to access marketing and the optimum consumers will get healthy and hygenic meat products from that services.

Cattle raising training

Sankalpa Agrotech is supported by सीप(Skill for Employment Project) and implemented in partnership with Shreenagar Agro farm(SAF), UKAid as a thematic partner.
The company empowers the commercial farmers and entrepreneurs for sustainable development by providing practical knowledge of livestock raising through innovative information, technology, services and input supply.

Livestock Vaccination

Vaccines reduce risks of getting disease by working with livestock body's natural defenses to build protection. We now have vaccines to prevent more than 10 life-threatening diseases helping animal of all ages live longer, healthier lives. We are providing vaccines to our customers like as: PPR, TT, ET, HS, BQ, ND, LMP, FMD, Pox, etc.

Low cost animal housing

Using simple bucket feeder to feed cattles as per their feed requirement. Locally available materials are used for the housing of the livestock. It is an efficient, simple,economic and farmer friendly technology.It is also promoted by Heifer International Nepal in project sites.

Mechanized milking process

Mechanization in dairy farms provide convenience and consistency of milking process. Milking of dairy animals is a critical activity and machine milking ensures consistency throughout the lactation of the animal with gentle handling of teats.

Passing on the gift

Each family that partners with us on a production and marketing project to passing on a cow, goats, seeds, etc. Whatever makes the most sense for them in their neighbour once they've established their business. It's truly a network effect. Families share the core components necessary to start a business and any other lessons that they've learned along the way. The company selects marginalized and backward family and give them the gifts which helps to start their own business, trust and creating a network.
